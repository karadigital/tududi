# Tududi Staff Training Tutorials

A structured tutorial series for onboarding staff onto the Tududi platform. Tutorials are organized into phases to support a gradual rollout.

---

## Phase 1 — Day One Onboarding (All Staff)

These three tutorials cover everything a new user needs to get started on their first day.

| # | Tutorial | Time | Description |
|---|---------|------|-------------|
| 1 | [Welcome — Your First 10 Minutes](01-welcome-your-first-10-minutes.md) | 10 min | Login, profile setup, interface tour, first task |
| 2 | [Managing Your Daily Work](02-managing-your-daily-work.md) | 15 min | Priorities, due dates, statuses, daily planning routine |
| 3 | [Organizing Work — Areas, Projects & Tags](03-organizing-work-areas-projects-tags.md) | 15 min | Company structure, projects, tags, notes |

## Phase 2 — First Week (All Staff)

These tutorials cover collaboration and workflow features needed for effective team use.

| # | Tutorial | Time | Description |
|---|---------|------|-------------|
| 4 | [Working Together — Assignment & Sharing](04-working-together-assignment-sharing.md) | 15 min | Task assignment, project sharing, permissions, subscriptions |
| 5 | [Staying in the Loop — Notifications](05-staying-in-the-loop-notifications.md) | 10 min | Notification channels, preferences, task summary emails |
| 6 | [Capturing Ideas Fast — The Inbox](06-capturing-ideas-fast-the-inbox.md) | 10 min | Quick capture, shorthand syntax, inbox processing |

## Phase 3 — First Month (Power Users)

Advanced features for staff who want to get more out of the platform.

| # | Tutorial | Time | Description |
|---|---------|------|-------------|
| 7 | [Recurring Tasks & Habits](07-recurring-tasks-and-habits.md) | 15 min | Recurrence patterns, habit tracking, streaks |
| 8 | [Custom Views — Your Personal Dashboard](08-custom-views-personal-dashboard.md) | 10 min | Saved filters, pinned views, view recipes |
| 9 | [Subtasks & Complex Work Breakdown](09-subtasks-complex-work-breakdown.md) | 10 min | Subtask hierarchies, attachments, event timeline |

## Phase 4 — Role-Specific

Tutorials targeted at specific roles within the organization.

| # | Tutorial | Time | Audience |
|---|---------|------|----------|
| 10 | [For Managers — Tracking Team Progress](10-for-managers-tracking-team-progress.md) | 15 min | Team leads, managers, department heads |

---

## Total Training Time

| Phase | Audience | Time |
|-------|----------|------|
| Phase 1 (Day One) | All staff | ~40 minutes |
| Phase 2 (Week One) | All staff | ~35 minutes |
| Phase 3 (Month One) | Power users | ~35 minutes |
| Phase 4 (Role-specific) | Managers | ~15 minutes |
| **Full series** | **All tutorials** | **~2 hours** |
